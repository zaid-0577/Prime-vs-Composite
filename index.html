Prime OR Composite

Prime Number: A prime number is a number which has exactly two factors i.e. ‘1’ and the number itself. example: 2,3,17 etc.

Composite Number: A composite number has more than two factors, which means apart from getting divided by 1 and the number itself, it can also be divided by at least one positive integer. example: 8,16,24 etc.

Note: 1 is neither prime nor a composite number. >
